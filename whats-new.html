v8June 2026 · current
Accounts, your own packing list, bilingual & a cleaner site
For everyone
  • English & French (CA) new — switch the whole site between English (CA) and Français (CA) anytime in ⚙ Settings. Your choice is saved on your device.
  • Pick who you are and get your own packing list — everything assigned to you, anything marked "all crew", plus your own custom items with optional quantities.
  • See the rest of the crew's lists too (read-only), so you know who still has things to pack.
  • Add your own food to the matrix, and edit the meal-plan days right on the cards.
  • "I bought this" on the shopping list — tap to show the crew you grabbed something, so nobody double-buys.
  • Add & auto-sort timeline points — new stops slot into the itinerary in chronological order automatically.
  • A cleaner, faster site — a Simplify mode that hides extra tabs, collapsible settings, a back-to-top button, clearer on/off switches, and better-looking menus across every theme.
Accounts, sign-in & roles
  • Sign in to sync live. The whole crew shares one trip in real time — gear, votes, costs, everything updates everywhere at once. Signed out, you're in a local, this-device mode.
  • First sign-in asks who you are — pick an existing crew member, add yourself as new crew, or just browse.
  • Roles: linking your name makes you a camper with full edit access; browsing makes you a visitor who can look but not change anything.
  • Edit or rename your own crew name anytime.
  • Your privacy: the email you sign in with is kept private — other crew members never see it.
  • Locked pages (Gear, Food, Shopping, Costs) are now only shown once you sign in.

  • In-app campsite maps new — open a full-screen map for each site, pinch to zoom, drag to pan, and save it to your phone for offline use.
