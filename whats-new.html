v8.3June 2026 · current
Smarter cost splitting & inline editing

Costs don't have to be split evenly anymore, and you can edit any expense right on the row.

✨ Features
  • Choose how a cost is split new — when a leader/admin adds an expense, pick Everyone (the default even split), Just payer (a personal cost that doesn't touch anyone else's balance), or Choose people… to split it evenly among only the people you tap.
  • Covers the tricky cases — "I paid for Qymar's dog fee, he pays me back", "I bought the kayak, Dylan & Baha split it", or "that was just my own snack". Each expense shows how it's split right under its name.
  • Edit costs inline new — tap ✎ to change the description, amount, category, who paid, and the split mode directly on the row, with Save / Cancel. No more pop-up boxes.
  • Accurate settle-up — balances and the "who pays who" transfers are recomputed for every split type and always add up to zero. "Per person" now shows the evenly-split amount only.
  • Settle-style toggle new — flip "Who pays who" between Grouped (everyone pays the main payer, who then balances with the others — matches "we front everything and settle at the end") and Fewest (minimal transfers). Saved with the trip and used by Copy for group chat.
  • One rental car — we're renting a single car and splitting it five ways; the header now reads 1–2 cars.
v8June 2026
Accounts, your own packing list & a cleaner site

The trip now has real accounts and roles, plus a lot of quality-of-life polish.

✨ Features
  • Pick who you are and get your own packing list — everything assigned to you, anything marked "all crew", plus your own items with optional quantities.
  • See the rest of the crew's lists (read-only) so you know who's still got things to pack.
  • Add your own food to the matrix, and edit the meal-plan days right on the card.
  • "I bought this" on the shopping list new — tap to show the crew you grabbed something, so nobody double-buys.
  • Add timeline points to the itinerary — they sort themselves into the right spot by time.
  • Pick your language new — the whole site in English (CA) or French (CA), saved per device.
  • A cleaner, faster site: Simplify mode, collapsible settings, a back-to-top button, and clearer on/off switches.
🔐 Sign-in, roles & security
  • Sign in to sync live — the whole crew shares one trip in real time; gear, votes, and costs update everywhere at once.
  • First time you sign in, a quick prompt asks if you're an existing crew member, a new one, or just browsing.
  • Roles: link your name to become a camper (full edit access), or browse as a visitor (view-only). Your role shows as a badge.
  • Edit or rename your own crew name any time.
  • Locked pages (Gear, Food, Shopping, Costs) are now sign-in only — they're hidden until you're in.
  • Your sign-in is private — linking your email to a crew name is never shown to other crew members.
v7June 2026
Personal packing, campsite maps & Simplify
✨ Features
  • Personal packing lists — a "who am I" picker gives you your own checklist (your gear + "all crew" items + personal extras) and a progress bar.
  • In-app campsite maps new — full-screen map per site: pinch to zoom, drag to pan, save to your phone for offline use.
  • Smart quick links — booking, info, directions, that town's weather, and the right fire-ban page all follow your chosen site.
  • Simplify mode — hides the secondary tabs for a cleaner phone view, keeping the essentials.
v4–v6June 2026
Sign-in, themes, offline mode & the rename
✨ Features
  • A dozen themes to choose from, with a fresh look that rotates each month — animated scenes on desktop, light versions on mobile.
  • Add it to your home screen for an app icon and full offline access; it re-syncs when you're back online.
  • Trip-readiness meters (crew / site / gear / shopping %), motion-on-scroll, and Compact mode.
  • Renamed to Camping 2026, and fixed stale deploys so everyone always gets the latest version.
🔐 Sign-in & security
  • Google sign-in arrived and now gates live access — signed-out is a local, device-only mode; signing in unlocks live sync, presence, and all themes.
  • Email magic-link sign-in as an alternative to Google — works everywhere, including the installed app.
  • Online presence counter showing how many of the crew are live right now.
  • Hardened, offline-resilient startup so the app is always responsive even if the network drops.
